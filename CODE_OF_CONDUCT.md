# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant][cc], version 2.1, as its code of
conduct. We pledge to make participation in PitStop a welcoming, respectful, and
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

[cc]: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

## Our standards

In short: be kind and constructive. Assume good faith, welcome newcomers, accept
feedback gracefully, and focus on what's best for the project and its community.
Disrespectful, harassing, or exclusionary behavior is not acceptable.

The full text of the standards and enforcement responsibilities lives in the
[Contributor Covenant 2.1][cc].

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces — issues, pull requests,
discussions, and any other channel — and when an individual is representing the
project in public.

## Reporting

Concerns about behavior may be reported privately to the project maintainer,
[@Livin21](https://github.com/Livin21). All reports will be reviewed and
handled confidentially. The maintainer is responsible for enforcement and may
take any action they deem appropriate, up to and including a ban from the
project's spaces.
